Property Description

Positioned in a quiet Riverhead enclave — craftsmanship, materiality and design integrity, with no detail overlooked.

A Monier Portuguese Classico antique clay tile roof paired with cedar fascia and a solid cedar front door creates a timeless, grounded presence. Outside, Antique Shahabad limestone paving adds a distinctly European character to Shafer-designed landscaped grounds.

Inside, 316m² of considered living unfolds across four bedrooms — including two masters — three bathrooms, and a double internal-access garage. Timber flooring runs throughout; custom Customtone cabinetry in kitchen, laundry and bathrooms. Burlington matt white tiles and Grohe tapware finish every wet room to an exacting standard.

A substantial family home that balances architectural integrity with everyday livability — in one of Riverhead's most peaceful residential pockets, minutes from motorway access, schools and village amenity.
